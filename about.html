About Us

Why Learn from the Rest…

When You can Learn from the Best!

Welcome to the Surfy Club! We offer you a range of lessons and camps to choose from. Whether you are looking for private lessons, group lessons, day surf camps, or even stand up paddle board lessons.

We have professional instructors highly trained in water safety and awareness, wave etiquette, board positioning, and wave knowledge who will encourage and guide you through your surfing progression.

Our Lesson

Our proven learn to surf method is a systematic approach to teaching people the proper mechanics of the act of surfing. Our lessons incorporate a land portion to learn about waves and test your balance and plenty of time in the water with our instructors to catch some waves!
